About me

I am a front-end developer, obtained a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science from UNIBI in Indonesia and a Master Degree in Business Management from VUB in Belgium. I am also a recent graduate of Coding Bootcamp Praha. Having front-end developing experience working for Lazada Indonesia and business developer for Essence of Bali Indonesia.

I am currently based in Rotterdam, the Netherlands.

I love using SASS and I am familiar with NPM ecosystem. I also like PostCSS for transforming styles with JS plugins.

Recently, I am learning to advance my skill to develop user interfaces in JavaScript and React and backend of my projects in PHP and Laravel.

I am open to learn new technologies and enjoy working with people who are as passionate about all web-related as me.

Just GET IN TOUCH with me if you'd like to find out more about my availability. I am currently looking to build on my skills and experience from both my studies and real world projects. My CV is available upon request. Thank you for reading.
